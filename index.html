About Us

AI Club of IGDTUW

Breathed into existence by the assiduous perseverance of the organizing student committee, backed by the unprecedented mentorship and superintendence of the supporting Faculty Coordinator, Prof. Arun Kumar Sharma, The pioneering AI Club of IGDTUW sets to sail with its perfervid mission and vision to inculcate a profound cognizance of the groundbreaking advancements of the field of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning while endeavouring to create diverse opportunities for aspiring innovators to achieve utilitarian experience and training in the practical applications of such trailblazing technologies and to design ingenious and sustainable innovations with far-reaching implementations contributing towards the universal revolutionization of mankind.
